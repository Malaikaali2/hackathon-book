# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Book

This repository contains the academic book "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: Embodied Intelligence, Simulation-to-Reality Workflows, and Modern AI-Robot Integration". This comprehensive resource covers the intersection of artificial intelligence and embodied systems, providing both theoretical foundations and practical implementations of intelligent robots that interact with the physical world.

## Overview

The book explores essential topics from robotic operating systems to vision-language-action models, targeting graduate students, researchers, and software engineers interested in the rapidly evolving field of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

## Structure

The content is organized into four core modules:
- **Module 1**: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)
- **Module 2**: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)
- **Module 3**: The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac)
- **Module 4**: Vision-Language-Action (VLA)

Plus a comprehensive capstone project: "The Autonomous Humanoid"
